📂 Complaint Categories

This system allows only verified students to submit complaints. Categories include:

  • 👨‍🏫 STAFF
  • 🏅 SPORTS
  • 🍽️ CANTEEN
  • 📝 EXAM
  • 🚰 WATER FACILITY
  • 📌 OTHER

🔍 How the Complaint System Works

  1. Your complaint is securely recorded with the current timestamp.
  2. Student verification is done via Registration Number, Department, and Year.
  3. Admin reviews and takes action on the complaint.
  4. You can track your complaint status via the "My Complaints" page.
  5. Once resolved, the complaint is marked and closed.

✅ Why Use This Complaint System?

  • 🔒 Safe & Secure: Your complaint is confidential and protected.
  • ⏱️ Timely Action: Complaints are reviewed and acted upon quickly.
  • 📊 Transparent Tracking: You can track your complaint status anytime.
  • ✅ Resolution Guaranteed: Admins ensure appropriate action is taken.
